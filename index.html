About Us

Jobs Institute of Technology & Management has been registered under the Government of India.We are working in different fields of Programme & Commercial Training sponsored by the Central Government by taking a vow to approach every class of Youth in the society. The Organization is certified by ISO 9001:2015 for providing quality trainning. The Institute & the Organization is also Registered under the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) is accredited to conduct the various fields of technical programs sponsored by both the Govt. and Pvt.JITM is an authorised study center of NIELIT,Tally,Unistal,Marg & Microsoft. Jobs Institute of Technology & Management the main function of the society is to provide higher technical education in nominal charges for every group of society of Urban & Rural areas all over India and get success in computer revolution which is the main dream of Indian Govt..
